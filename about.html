History

Kelsey Hamilton founded type-in in the year 2015 with a vision to sell old and vintage typewriters to the world. With starting with two employees in her own office, she has come a long way. Over the years, our company has grown and has become one of the world's largest seller of typewriters with about 500k customers. In 2019, we received the "Best vintage product seller" award from the prestigious industry publication TOI Magazine. Today, our team consists of over 20 dedicated professionals who are committed to providing our customers with the best possible products and support. We are proud of our journey so far, and we look forward to continuing to innovate and grow in the years to come.
